In making the transition, I made six different images with four more being copies for the transition to come to a full circle. In making the animation, I labeled the id as "beauty". On the style screen of the html, I typed in the url link for the image. On the css, I typed down all of the adequate detials such as border at a solid black 1px, height at 620px, weight at 495px, and linking the animation to the term "beauty" at 10 steps under 1.2 seconds per cycle, infinitely. On top of that, the @keyframes was at 100% with the background positioning at -5000px.

#beauty {
background: url("img/transition.png") 0 0 no-repeat;
border:1px solid black;
height: 620px;
width: 495px;
margin-left: auto;
margin-right: auto;
animation: beauty 1.2s steps(10) infinite;
}

@keyframes beauty {
100% {background-position: -5000px;}
}
